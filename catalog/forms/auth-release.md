## When to use
When a student authorizes sharing their information with an approved outside agency or person (for example, an employer or a partner organization).

## When NOT to use
- NOT for the WV Department of Health Services — same kind of release, but DoHS has its own scoped form: [DoHS Release of Information](./dohs-release.md).
