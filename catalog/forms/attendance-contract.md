## When to use
During orientation, when a new student commits to a regular attendance schedule that meets their required weekly/monthly hours.

## When NOT to use
- NOT how daily attendance is recorded or hours are proven — that is the [Student Sign-in Sheet](./sign-in-sheet.md).
- NOT attendance proof for the Ready to Work certificate — that is [Ready to Work Attendance Verification](./rtw-attendance.md).

## Related
A commitment made at orientation; the student's actual hours are then tracked daily on the [Student Sign-in Sheet](./sign-in-sheet.md).
