## When to use
When an instructor is walking a new student through orientation — this checklist confirms that every required step, signature, and form has been completed before the student starts class.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for the master program-wide orientation checklist document — see the [SPOKES Checklist for Student Orientation](../documents/spokes-checklist-for-student-orientation.md) document for the broader onboarding reference.
- NOT a substitute for any individual form — each item on this checklist (profile, releases, contract, etc.) is a separate form the student must actually sign.
