## When to use
Every class day, to record who attended. This is the record an instructor uses to total each student's hours for the monthly timesheet.

## When NOT to use
- NOT the one-time commitment to attend — that is the [Personal Attendance Contract](./attendance-contract.md).
- NOT the monthly state timesheet itself — daily hours are totaled onto [DFA-TS-12 Activity Tracking](./dfa-ts-12.md).
- NOT attendance proof for the Ready to Work certificate — that is [Ready to Work Attendance Verification](./rtw-attendance.md).

## Related
Daily hours recorded here are compiled onto the monthly [DFA-TS-12 Activity Tracking](./dfa-ts-12.md) timesheet.
