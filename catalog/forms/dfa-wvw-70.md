## When to use
When documentation is needed to confirm that a student is actively participating in the WV Works program — for example, when the state or a caseworker requires participation verification.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for reporting a student's monthly hours — that is the [DFA-TS-12 Activity Tracking](./dfa-ts-12.md) timesheet.
- NOT for requesting support services — that is the [DFA-WVW-25 Support Services](./dfa-wvw-25.md) form.

## Related
Part of the WV Works DoHS form set: [DFA-TS-12 Activity Tracking](./dfa-ts-12.md) (monthly hours) and [DFA-WVW-25 Support Services](./dfa-wvw-25.md) (support service requests).
