## When to use
During orientation, when a student must be given the required federal notice of their equal-opportunity rights in the program.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for general program rules or student expectations — those are in [Rights and Responsibilities](./rights-responsibilities.md).
- NOT a release-of-information form — for information sharing see [Authorization for Release of Information](./auth-release.md) or [DoHS Release of Information](./dohs-release.md).

## Related
Provided at orientation alongside [Rights and Responsibilities](./rights-responsibilities.md) as part of the required federal compliance acknowledgments.
