## When to use
When a student needs to submit a formal request for WV Works support services — for example, to get reimbursed for transportation costs or to request childcare assistance.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for learning about what support services are available — first read the [Support Services Fact Sheet](./support-services-fact-sheet.md), then come back to this form to request them.
- NOT for monthly hours tracking — that is the [DFA-TS-12 Activity Tracking](./dfa-ts-12.md).
- NOT for participation verification — that is the [DFA-WVW-70 Participant Form](./dfa-wvw-70.md).

## Related
The [Support Services Fact Sheet](./support-services-fact-sheet.md) explains what services are available; this form is used to actually request them. Related DoHS forms: [DFA-TS-12 Activity Tracking](./dfa-ts-12.md) and [DFA-WVW-70 Participant Form](./dfa-wvw-70.md).
