## When to use
During orientation (or whenever needed), when a student is asked whether they consent to having their photo or video used by the program in materials such as newsletters, social media, or grant reports.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for sharing student records or personal information with outside agencies — that is the [Authorization for Release of Information](./auth-release.md) or [DoHS Release of Information](./dohs-release.md).
- NOT a general information-release form — this is specifically about images and video.
