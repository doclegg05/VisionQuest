## When to use
At the end of each month, to compile and submit a WV Works student's total activity hours to DoHS. The daily hours that feed this sheet come from the [Student Sign-in Sheet](./sign-in-sheet.md).

## When NOT to use
- NOT for documenting daily attendance — that is the [Student Sign-in Sheet](./sign-in-sheet.md).
- NOT for verifying that a student is participating in WV Works — that is the [DFA-WVW-70 Participant Form](./dfa-wvw-70.md).
- NOT for requesting support services such as transportation or childcare — that is the [DFA-WVW-25 Support Services](./dfa-wvw-25.md).

## Related
Daily hours are recorded on the [Student Sign-in Sheet](./sign-in-sheet.md) and compiled here each month. Related DoHS forms: [DFA-WVW-70 Participant Form](./dfa-wvw-70.md) (participation verification) and [DFA-WVW-25 Support Services](./dfa-wvw-25.md) (support service requests).
