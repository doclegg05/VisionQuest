## When to use
When a student is completing the Ready to Work certification process and needs their attendance record verified to satisfy the certificate requirement.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for recording daily classroom attendance — that is the [Student Sign-in Sheet](./sign-in-sheet.md).
- NOT the student's one-time attendance commitment made at orientation — that is the [Personal Attendance Contract](./attendance-contract.md).
- NOT for verifying WV Works participation hours on the state timesheet — that is [DFA-TS-12 Activity Tracking](./dfa-ts-12.md).

## Related
Daily hours tracked on the [Student Sign-in Sheet](./sign-in-sheet.md) feed into this verification; the attendance commitment is made up front on the [Personal Attendance Contract](./attendance-contract.md).
