## When to use
When a student is preparing for job interviews and needs to structure answers to behavioral questions ("tell me about a time when...") — the worksheet explains each STAR element and has the student draft real examples from their work, school, or life experience.

## When NOT to use
- NOT live practice — for a simulated back-and-forth mock interview, use the ChatGPT Interview Practice Prompts.

## Related
