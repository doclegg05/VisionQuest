## When to use
When someone asks which certifications a SPOKES student can earn and HOW each one is issued — Wufoo request forms, Certiport class setup and proctoring, WorkKeys exam scheduling, and which certificates are limited to SPOKES or SPOKES-blended students. Per this document, the NCRC WorkKeys exams are Workplace Documents, Applied Math, and Graphic Literacy.

## When NOT to use
- NOT the corpus document "SPOKES Modules 2025" — that is teacher-side module planning; this is the certification-access catalog.
- NOT the prompt-level certification list hardcoded in `src/lib/sage/knowledge-base.ts` (SPOKES_KNOWLEDGE) — that list remains Sage's always-on baseline until the post-sync reconciliation in `docs/runbooks/career-grounding-sync.md`; when the two disagree, this document is the newer curated source and the discrepancy should be surfaced, not blended.
- Issuance contacts name specific staff — confirm currency before a student relies on a named contact.
