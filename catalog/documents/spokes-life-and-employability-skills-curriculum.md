## When to use
When someone asks what a Life and Employability Skills module covers, what its learning outcomes are, or which module addresses a student's need (goal setting, problem solving and decision making, coping with change, and the rest of the module set).

## When NOT to use
- NOT the one-page corpus document "SPOKES Life and Employability Skills Curriculum Module Descriptor" or the "SPOKES Life and Employability Module Rubric Record" — those are the summary descriptor and the scoring record; this is the full curriculum master.
- NOT a student worksheet — module handouts (SMART goals, career planning) are separate documents.
