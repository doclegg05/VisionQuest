## When to use
When preparing for or conducting a new student's orientation — this is the master reference that ensures every required form, policy acknowledgment, and program introduction is covered.

## When NOT to use
- NOT a substitute for the individual forms — each item listed here (profile, releases, attendance contract, etc.) still needs to be completed separately as its own signed document.
