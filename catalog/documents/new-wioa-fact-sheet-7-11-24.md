## When to use
**Instructor / policy context** (Phase A Q4) — when an instructor needs WIOA funding facts (ITAs, scholarships, OJT, mileage) or Region 1 WorkForce WV career-planner contacts by county. Not a general student RAG handout.

## When NOT to use
- NOT as a student-facing always-on resource — audience is instructor/policy.
- NOT the referral itself — starting a referral uses the [WIOA Referral Form](./wioa-referral-form.md) (instructor-only).
- Eligibility depends on household income and work status and is decided by the WIOA career planner — present funding as something to ASK about, never as promised money.
- Contact names/numbers are Region 1 and dated 7/11/24 — confirm currency before relying on them.

## Related
Start a referral with the [WIOA Referral Form](./wioa-referral-form.md). Check which local occupations WIOA-funded training targets in the [Region 1 Demand Occupation List 2024](./region-1-demand-occupation-list-2024.md).
