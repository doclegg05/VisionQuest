## When to use
When a student asks how to pay for job training, what WIOA offers (ITAs, scholarships, OJT, mileage reimbursement), or who to contact — the sheet lists the Region 1 WorkForce WV career planners by county.

## When NOT to use
- NOT the referral itself — starting a referral uses the WIOA Referral Form.
- Eligibility depends on household income and work status and is decided by the WIOA career planner — present funding as something to ASK about, never as promised money.
- Contact names/numbers are Region 1 and dated 7/11/24 — confirm currency before a student relies on them.
