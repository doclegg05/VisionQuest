## When to use
When a student wants realistic interview practice with feedback — the prompt makes an AI conduct a full entry-level mock interview, then report what went well, what to improve, and how to strengthen answers.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for structuring answers on paper first — that is the STAR Interview Worksheet.
- NOT career discovery — self-discovery prompts are the Career Discovery Prompts document.

## Related
Draft structured answers first with the [STAR Interview Worksheet](./star-interview-worksheet.md). For AI-guided career self-discovery (rather than interview prep) see [Career Discovery Prompts](./career-discovery-prompts.md).
