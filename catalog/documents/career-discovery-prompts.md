## When to use
When a student feels unsure about career direction and wants a guided, low-pressure self-discovery conversation. The guide's sectioned prompts (getting started, identifying strengths, career matching) pace the student one question at a time, and results are saved and brought to class.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for interview practice — that is the ChatGPT Interview Practice Prompts document.
- NOT the official ECP process — discovery output feeds the plan; it does not replace it.
