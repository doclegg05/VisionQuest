## When to use
When a student (or instructor supporting a student) needs help using the Aztec PLUS platform — finding lessons, taking tests, tracking progress, or troubleshooting the system.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for help with logging in or internet access specifically — that is the [Internet Student Handout](./internet-student-handout-plus-version-9-0-1.md), which covers login and browser setup.

## Related
Use alongside the [Internet Student Handout](./internet-student-handout-plus-version-9-0-1.md) (login and internet access) and the [Aztec PLUS platform](../platforms/aztec.md) overview.
