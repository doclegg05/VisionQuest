## When to use
When a student has explored career interests and is ready to write them down as a concrete plan — three target occupations, the education or training each requires, a skill-development plan with timelines, and the people who will hold them accountable.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for writing the goal itself — the SMART goal mechanics live in Handout 4.
- NOT for raw exploration — if the student doesn't yet know what interests them, start with the CFWV worksheet or the Career Discovery Prompts.
