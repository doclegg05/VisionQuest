## When to use
When a student is ready to write (or revise) their official Education and Career Plan — the program plan of record that ties assessment results, career interests, barriers, and next steps to a terminal outcome of employment or post-secondary transfer. Sage should walk the student through sections one at a time and hand them the Open button via present_form / document link.

## When NOT to use
- NOT the CFWV Career Exploration Worksheet — that is exploration practice, not the plan of record.
- NOT the teacher-filed ECP under Handbook Appendix (same PDF content, TEACHER audience for staff filing). Prefer this student copy when coaching a student.
- NOT for WIOA referral paperwork — that stays instructor-only.
