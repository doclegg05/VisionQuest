## When to use
When a student is weighing career choices against local labor-market demand, or checking whether a training goal (LPN, welder, HVAC tech, pharmacy tech, CDL driver, etc.) is in demand in WIOA Region 1. Demand-occupation status also matters for WIOA-funded training (ITAs favor demand occupations).

## When NOT to use
- The list covers calendar year 2024 only — check for a newer list before presenting it as current.
- Region 1 (southern WV) only — it is not a statewide or national demand list.
