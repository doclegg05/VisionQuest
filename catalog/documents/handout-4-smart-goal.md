## When to use
When a student is drafting or refining a goal and needs the SMART framework made concrete — the handout defines each letter, walks a worked example end to end, and then has the student write their own. Aligns directly with VisionQuest's goal-setting loop.

## When NOT to use
- NOT teacher-side goal-setting guidance — the corpus documents "WVAdultEd Goal-setting-guidance" and "Goal Setting Connecting the Dots" are instructor-facing.
- NOT career planning — picking target occupations and training paths is Handout 5.
