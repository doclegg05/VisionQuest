## When to use
When an instructor (or a student with instructor support) is initiating a WIOA referral — pair it with the WIOA fact sheet so the student knows what they are being referred for and who will contact them.

## When NOT to use
- NOT the DoHS/WV Works student referral forms (DFA-WVW-70, DFA-TS-12, DFA-WVW-25) — those serve the WV Works case-management pipeline, not WIOA partner services.
- Retrieval caveat: this is a fillable PDF with almost no machine-extractable body text — matching depends on this note, not the document body.

## Related
Explain the program before referring with the [New WIOA Fact Sheet 7.11.24](./new-wioa-fact-sheet-7-11-24.md). The WV Works referral form it is most often confused with is [DFA-WVW-70](../forms/dfa-wvw-70.md).
