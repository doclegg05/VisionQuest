## When to use
When an **instructor** (instructor-designated profile only) is initiating a WIOA partner referral. Sage may retrieve this only in instructor sessions — never for student-facing coaching.

## When NOT to use
- NOT for student profiles — students must not see or retrieve this form (Phase A Q3, 2026-07-23).
- NOT the DoHS/WV Works student referral forms (DFA-WVW-70, DFA-TS-12, DFA-WVW-25) — those serve the WV Works case-management pipeline, not WIOA partner services.
- Retrieval caveat: this is a fillable PDF with almost no machine-extractable body text — matching depends on this note, not the document body.
