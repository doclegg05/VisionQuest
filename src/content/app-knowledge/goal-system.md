# Goal System

## How Goals Work in VisionQuest

VisionQuest uses a layered goal system to help you turn a big dream into daily actions. Goals are not just something you write down once — they are meant to be reviewed and updated regularly as you make progress.

## Goal Levels

Your goals are organized into five levels, from broadest to most specific:

- **BHAG (Big Hairy Audacious Goal)** — Your long-term vision. This is the big dream that drives everything else, like "become a medical assistant" or "start my own business."
- **Monthly Goal** — What you will accomplish this month to move toward your BHAG.
- **Weekly Goal** — What you will focus on this week.
- **Daily Goal** — The most important thing you will do today.
- **Tasks** — Specific action steps you can check off as you go.

## Setting Goals with Sage

Sage guides you through goal-setting during your conversations. Rather than filling out a form, you talk with Sage about what matters to you. Sage helps you discover your direction, then breaks it down into smaller goals you can act on. Sage only records a goal when you have clearly stated or agreed to it — nothing is created without your input.

## Goal Confirmation by Teachers

After you set a goal, your teacher reviews and confirms it. This confirmation step makes sure your goals are realistic and aligned with the SPOKES program. Your teacher can also help you restate or adjust goals if needed. A goal is not final until a teacher has confirmed it.

## Goals and Your Learning Pathway

Once your goals are confirmed, VisionQuest connects them to recommended courses and certifications. For example, if your BHAG involves office work, the system may suggest Microsoft Office Specialist certification. Your teacher makes the final call on which pathway fits your goals.

## Reviewing Goals

Goals should be reviewed regularly. If a goal becomes stale or has not been looked at recently, your teacher will be alerted so they can check in with you. You can always update your goals through the Goals page or by talking with Sage.
