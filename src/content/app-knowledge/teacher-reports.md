# Teacher Reports

## Readiness Reports

Readiness reports show how close each student is to completing the program. Every student has a readiness score from 0 to 100 that reflects their progress across multiple dimensions: goal completion, orientation status, certifications earned, portfolio items, and attendance patterns.

## Monthly KPI Dashboard

The monthly KPI dashboard gives you a snapshot of class-level metrics for the reporting period. It covers enrollment, active participation, certification completion rates, and goal-setting activity. These numbers help you see whether the class as a whole is on track.

## Academic Effectiveness Metrics

Academic effectiveness metrics track whether students are actually moving from goals to action to evidence. Key indicators include the percentage of students with confirmed goals, the rate of pathway assignments, and how quickly evidence is attached after work is completed.

## How to Interpret Readiness Scores

A readiness score combines several factors into one number. A score above 80 generally means the student is close to Ready to Work. Scores between 50 and 80 indicate progress but with gaps to address. Below 50 means significant work remains. Look at the individual dimensions in the student detail view to see exactly where a student is strong or falling behind.

## Class Progress Tracking

You can track overall class progress from the Students page and from reports. The class overview shows aggregate completion percentages, and the intervention queue highlights where individual students are stalling. Together, these views give you both the big picture and the specific actions to take.

## Accessing Reports

Reports are available through Program Setup under the Reports section. Monthly readiness reports can be generated on demand and reflect the current state of your class data.
