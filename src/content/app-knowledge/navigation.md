# Navigating VisionQuest

## Sidebar Navigation

The sidebar on the left is your main way to get around VisionQuest. On mobile devices, navigation appears as a bottom bar with the most important items and a "More" menu for the rest.

## Student Navigation

As a student, your sidebar includes these sections:

- **Home** — Your main dashboard with your readiness score, suggested next actions, and progress overview.
- **Orientation** — Where you complete your onboarding forms and documents. This disappears from the sidebar once you finish orientation.
- **Goals** — Where you set, review, and track your goals at every level, from your big long-term dream down to today's tasks.
- **Learning** — Your hub for courses, certifications, and learning platforms. Everything related to what you are studying lives here.
- **Portfolio** — Build your resume, upload work samples, and manage shareable proof of your achievements.
- **Career** — Browse job opportunities and events related to your career path.
- **Advising** — View your appointments, tasks, and alerts from your instructor.

## Secondary Items

You also have access to **Vision Board**, **Files**, and **Resources** in the sidebar. Vision Board is a space for inspiration images and ideas. Files is for uploading and managing documents. Resources contains helpful links and reference materials.

## Sage — Your AI Coach

Sage is available as a floating chat button, not a sidebar item. You can open Sage from any page to ask questions, get help with goals, or talk through next steps. On the full chat page, Sage remembers your conversation history.

## Teacher Navigation

Teachers see three main items: **Students** (the intervention queue and student list), **Classes** (manage class rosters), and **Program Setup** (orientation templates, learning content, career resources, and reports).

## Finding What You Need

If you are not sure where something is, ask Sage. Sage knows the layout of VisionQuest and can point you to the right page. You can also check your Home dashboard — it surfaces the most important next actions for you.
