# Employment Portfolio

## What Is the Portfolio?

Your portfolio is where you collect and present proof of everything you have accomplished in the SPOKES program. It is designed to show employers that you are ready to work. Think of it as your professional showcase.

## Portfolio Tabs

The Portfolio page has three sections:

- **Portfolio Items** — Upload and organize documents like work samples, certificates, reference letters, and any other evidence of your skills.
- **Resume Builder** — Create a resume directly in VisionQuest. The builder walks you through adding your experience, skills, and education so you can generate a polished resume.
- **Shareable Proof** — View and share your credentials and Credly badges with employers or anyone who needs to verify your achievements.

## What Goes in a Complete Portfolio

A strong employment portfolio typically includes:

- A current resume
- Earned certifications (IC3, MOS, WorkKeys, etc.)
- Work samples that demonstrate your skills
- Professional references
- A cover letter
- Any additional documents that show your readiness for employment

## How to Upload Documents

From the Portfolio Items tab, you can upload files directly. Supported file types include PDFs, images, and common document formats. Each item gets a label so you can keep things organized.

## Certifications in Your Portfolio

When you earn a certification, it can appear in your portfolio as shareable proof. The Shareable Proof tab lets you connect your Credly account to display digital badges, making it easy for employers to verify your credentials online.

## For Teachers

Teachers can view a student's portfolio from the student detail view under the Progress tab. This helps you see what proof of progress a student has collected and identify any gaps before they complete the program.
