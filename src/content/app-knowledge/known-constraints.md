# Known Constraints

## First Load May Be Slow

VisionQuest runs on a free hosting tier that puts the server to sleep after a period of inactivity. The first time you visit after a quiet period, the app may take 30 to 60 seconds to load. After that initial load, everything runs at normal speed. If the page seems stuck, just wait a moment — it is waking up.

## Sage Response Time

Sage (your AI coach) processes your messages through an AI service, which can take a few seconds to respond. If Sage seems slow, it is thinking through your question — give it a moment. Longer or more detailed questions may take slightly more time.

## Web-Based Application

VisionQuest is a web application that works in any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. It also works on phones and tablets. There is no app to download; just visit the website and log in.

## No Offline Mode

VisionQuest requires an internet connection to work. Your data is stored securely in the cloud, so you need to be online to access your goals, portfolio, orientation forms, and conversations with Sage. If you lose connection, save your work and try again when you are back online.

## File Uploads

When uploading files (for orientation forms, portfolio items, or other documents), supported formats include PDFs, images, and common document types. Very large files may take longer to upload depending on your internet speed.
